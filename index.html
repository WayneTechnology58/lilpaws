About Me

Hi, I'm Lille. I've been a professional dog groomer for four years, and I absolutely love what I do. For me, grooming isn't just about keeping dogs looking their best, it's about making sure they feel comfortable, relaxed, and cared for throughout the process.

Whether your pup needs a tidy trim, a bubbly bath, or a little extra pampering, I take the time to make each session a positive experience. I focus on creating a calm environment where every dog can feel at ease and leave looking (and feeling) their very best.
